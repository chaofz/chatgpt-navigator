ChatGPT Navigator Settings

Customize your navigation sidebar experience

Appearance

How the navigation sidebar looks on ChatGPT

Auto detects light or dark mode from the ChatGPT page and updates when you change the app theme.

Scroll Behavior

Control how the page scrolls during conversations

When enabled, the page won't automatically scroll to the bottom when ChatGPT generates a new response. You can still scroll manually.

Toolbar

Buttons at the top of the navigation sidebar

Collapse always stays visible. Pin remembers scroll position; back returns to it.

Click Behavior

Choose how clicking on outline items works

When enabled, questions and responses are combined. Clicking goes to the question.

Display Limit

Control how many questions are shown in the outline

The outline will be scrollable if there are more questions than this limit.
