# Privacy Policy for ChatGPT Navigator

**Last Updated:** June 20, 2026

Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how **ChatGPT Navigator** (the "Extension") accesses and processes data.

---

### 1. No Server-Side Collection or Transmission
The Extension is a purely client-side utility. **We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data, chat history, or browsing activity to any external servers.** 
All operations are performed locally in your browser sandbox on your device.

---

### 2. Locally Accessed Data
To provide its functionality, the Extension reads and monitors the following data locally within your browser tab:

*   **Website Content (ChatGPT DOM):** The Extension reads the text and structural headings of the active ChatGPT website (`chatgpt.com` or `chat.openai.com`) solely to generate the navigation outline sidebar. This data is kept strictly in-memory and is destroyed when the tab is closed.
*   **User Activity (Scroll & Clicks):** The Extension monitors the active tab's scroll position and navigation button clicks to highlight active outline headings and support the scroll-lock and scroll-pinning features.

---

### 3. Storage
The Extension uses the `chrome.storage.sync` API to save your settings (such as sidebar theme selection, visible buttons, and custom behaviors). These settings are synchronized across your Google account devices by Chrome and contain no personal identifier information or conversation content.

---

### 4. Third-Party Sharing
Because we do not collect or store your data, **we do not sell, rent, trade, or share your information** with any third-party advertisers, companies, or analytics providers.

---

### 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.

---

### 6. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the GitHub repository issue tracker.
