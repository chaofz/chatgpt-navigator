# 🚀 ChatGPT Navigator: Professional Sidebar & Automation

**Transform your ChatGPT experience with a sleek, minimalist navigation sidebar. Stay focused, navigate faster, and automate your workflow.**

ChatGPT Navigator is the essential productivity companion for power users. It automatically generates a Table of Contents for your conversations, provides smart scroll controls, and allows you to drive ChatGPT directly from your browser's address bar.

---

### 📑 Key Features

*   **Automatic Table of Contents**: Instantly jump between questions and responses with a clickable outline.
*   **📍 One-Click Pinning**: Save your current scroll position and return to it instantly—no more endless scrolling back and forth.
*   **🔒 Smart Scroll Lock**: Prevent annoying auto-scrolling to the bottom while reading or copying code from long responses.
*   **⚡ URL Automation**: Launch ChatGPT with pre-filled prompts, auto-submit, and forced model selection (Thinking/Instant) via URL hash parameters.
*   **🎨 Ultra-Minimalist Design**: A "flat" UI that feels like a native part of ChatGPT. Choose from Auto, Light, or Dark modes.
*   **⌨️ Power Shortcuts**: Use `Option + Arrows` to navigate the outline and `Cmd/Ctrl + Enter` to submit prompts.

---

### 🛠️ How to Use

1.  **Navigate**: Open any ChatGPT conversation. The sidebar appears automatically on the right.
2.  **Jump**: Click any item in the Table of Contents to scroll to that message instantly.
3.  **Lock**: Click the **Padlock** icon to freeze the screen while ChatGPT is typing.
4.  **Pin**: Click the **Map-Pin** icon to save a spot. Click it again to jump back.
5.  **Automate**: Use `https://chatgpt.com/#prompt=Hello&autoSubmit=1` to launch a task from your address bar.
